Few Accomplishments in YT Studio

In 2022, a group of students from the University of Natural Science and Bach Khoa University joined forces to establish a small game development studio. When they approached me to lead their team, I was intrigued by their enthusiasm and potential. As a team, we embarked on our journey, starting off with modest beginnings. The initial stages were challenging, and progress was slow. However, with my guidance, training, and mentorship, we gradually gained momentum. Over time, the team evolved and matured. What began as a modest endeavor has now transformed into a capable studio capable of creating complex games. I have full confidence that this team will continue to thrive and grow in the ever-evolving world of game development.
